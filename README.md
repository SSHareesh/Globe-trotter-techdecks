## ğŸš€ What is GlobeTrotter?

GlobeTrotter is a modern web application that combines the power of AI (Groq Llama 3.1), real-time travel APIs, and a vibrant community to make travel planning seamless and inspiring. Whether you're a solo explorer or a family planner, GlobeTrotter helps you:

- **Discover** trending destinations and hidden gems worldwide
- **Plan** trips with AI-optimized itineraries, real-time flight and hotel data
- **Book** flights and hotels directly from your itinerary
- **Share** experiences and tips with a global travel community
- **Get instant help** from an AI chatbot for all your travel questions

## âœ¨ Key Features

- **AI Chatbot**: Get instant, travel-focused answers and recommendations powered by Groq Llama 3.1.
- **Smart Search**: Real-time city/airport search with Amadeus and Pexels images.
- **Trip Builder**: Multi-step wizard for creating trips, selecting attractions, and booking travel.
- **AI Itinerary**: Hour-by-hour, day-by-day plans generated by AI, tailored to your interests.
- **Community Feed**: Share posts, like, comment, and connect with other travelers.
- **Calendar View**: Visualize all your trips in a monthly calendar.
- **Admin Dashboard**: Analytics, user management, and content moderation for admins.
- **Modern UI**: Responsive, accessible, and visually engaging design using React, TypeScript, and TailwindCSS.
